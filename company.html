Company

CAAPI.AI (Computer Aided/Applied Physics Investigation) is a next- generation R&D company developing advanced tools and intelligent systems to tackle some of the most complex challenges in defense, aerospace, and engineering. We bring together cutting-edge research and real-world requirements to build technologies that are not only innovative but also deployable in critical environments. Our mission is to blend physics, algorithms, and artificial intelligence to create robust, real-time, and scalable solutions. From high-fidelity simulations to AI-enhanced battlefield systems, CAAPI is building the digital backbone for the next era of engineering and defense innovation.
